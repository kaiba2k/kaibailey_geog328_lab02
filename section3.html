Other Athletics at the University of Washington

Click a sport to read a short description.

UW Huskies football competes in the NCAA Division I FBS and has a long tradition in the Pac-12 (now Pac-12 legacy). The program is known for strong fan support and a competitive schedule.

The men's basketball team plays in the NCAA Division I and regularly competes in conference play and national tournaments, developing NBA-level talent.

UW women's basketball is a strong program with NCAA tournament appearances and a focus on player development and academic success.

The Huskies softball program competes at a high level in the BIG-10, with strong regional presence and postseason appearances.

Rowing is a historic and successful sport at UW, with national championships and Olympic-level athletes emerging from the program.

These programs develop distance and field athletes who compete at conference and national meets, often producing All-Americans.

Men's and club hockey opportunities exist in the region; the university supports club-level competition and local fan engagement.
